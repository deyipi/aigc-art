#### Therapy System

To validate the therapatic effect of AiiA, we validated the anti-biofilm activity of AiiA protein by culturing the protein together with *Pseudomonas aeruginosa* and using crystal violet staining method. 

##### Validation of Anti-Biofilm Activity

AiiA protein was dissolved in lysis buffer (10 mM Tris-HCl, 1 mM EDTA) to yield a 20 μg/mL stock solution. The stock was subsequently diluted with M63 medium to prepare working solutions at final concentrations of 1, 2, 5, and 10 μg/mL. We activated *P. aeruginosa* freeze-dried powder and inoculated it onto TSA medium. Then we picked single colonies and inoculated them into TSB medium at a 1:50 dilution ratio, then transferred them to M63 medium. In accordance with the experimental design, *P. aeruginosa* suspensions, and pre-prepared AiiA protein solutions were added to a 96-well microplate. After 24 hours of cultivation, we stained them with 0.1% crystal violet staining solution (Biosharp, China) at the corresponding time points. We dissolved it with 30% acetic acid water solution and measured the absorbance at 550nm using a microplate reader.
