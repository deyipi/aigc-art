#### Adhesion System

To validate the interaction between our adhesion target, HopQ, and our adhesion molecule, C1ND, we established a series of experiments covering protein expression in *E. coli*, purification, and subsequent interaction assays including co-immunoprecipitation and immunocytochemistry.

##### Expression and Analysis of C1ND

###### Plasmid Transformation

Plasmids encoding the adhesion system components were transformed into chemically competent *E. coli* DH5α cells (Juhemei, China). Single colonies were cultured, and plasmids were then extracted using the Plasmid Miniprep Kit (Beyotime, China). The plasmid is then transformed into *Saccharomyces cerevisiae* BY4741 using the Competent Cell Preparation and Transformation Kit for *Saccharomyces Cerevisiae*(Beyotime, China).The transformed culture was plated onto SD-Ura selective agar plates and incubated in a 30°C constant temperature incubator for 2 days until single colonies appeared.     

###### Protein Expression

Pick a single positive colony and inoculate it into 10 mL of the SC-Ura+Glu activation medium overnight. Inoculate an appropriate volume of the culture into 100 mL of the SC-Ura selective medium to achieve an initial OD<sub>600</sub> between 0.2 and 0.4. Add 10 mL of the 30% galactose-raffinose mixture(Macklin, China).Induce at 30°C with shaking at 250 rpm for 18-20 hours.

##### Expression and Analysis of HopQ

###### Plasmid Transformation

Plasmids encoding the adhesion system components were transformed into chemically competent *E. coli* DH5α cells (Juhemei, China). Single colonies were cultured, and plasmids were then extracted using the Plasmid Miniprep Kit (Beyotime, China). The expression plasmid was transformed into *E. coli* BL21(DE3) competent cells (Beyotime, China).

###### Protein Expression

A 10 mL overnight culture of the transformed BL21(DE3) was inoculated into 300 mL of LB medium with 100 µg/mL ampicillin. The culture was grown at 37°C, 200 RPM, until OD₆₀₀ reached 0.5-0.6. Expression was induced with 0.5 mM IPTG for 14-16 hours.

###### Protein Extraction and Purification

Centrifuge the cell culture at 5000 xg, 4°C, for 10 min. Discard the supernatant. Resuspend the pellet in LB medium and repeat the centrifugation step. Calculate the cell wet weight by weigh the empty tube and the tube containing the cell pellet. For every gram of the cell pellet, add 30 mL of lysis buffer which contains 60 mg Lysozyme (Beyotime, China), 30 mL Active Bacterial Protein Extraction Reagent (Beyotime, China), 300 µL 100x His-Tag Compatible Protease Inhibitor Cocktail (Beyotime, China), and 6 µL BeyoZonase Super Nuclease (Beyotime, China).Using BeyoMag™ His-tag Protein Purification Agarose Magnetic Beads (IDA-Ni) (Beyotime, China) to purify HopQ protein.

##### Co-Immunoprecipitation (Co-IP) and Western Blot Analysis

###### Protein-Yeast Interaction
*S. cerevisiae* cells expressing C1ND were cultured to an OD₆₀₀ of 1.0-2.0,  Following incubation, harvest the yeast cells by low-speed centrifugation at 3,000 x g for 5 min at 4 °C, discard the supernatant. Wash the yeast cell pellet twice with washing buffer to remove residual medium. Resuspend the cells by adding 450 µL of washing buffer to each tube of yeast pellet. Add 5–10 µg of purified HopQ protein to each yeast tube. Incubate all tubes on a rotating shaker at 4 °C with gentle agitation for 2−4 hours.

###### Yeast Cell Lysis

For each 50mg cell pellet, add 500 µL of lysis buffer which contains 1.4 µL of β-mercaptoethanol (BME), 7 µL of 100x Protease Inhibitor Cocktail, 2000 U of Lyticase. Resuspend the cell pellet by gentle pipetting.

The final soluble protein sample will be analyzed via SDS-PAGE and Western Blotting to confirm protein identity and quantity. Samples will then be loaded onto the gel and separated by electrophoresis according to standard procedures.

##### Immunocytofluorescence (ICF) Assay

*S. cerevisiae* cells expressing C1ND (and empty vector controls) were cultured, harvested, and fixed with Immune Staining Fixation Solution (Beyotime, China). The fixed cells were adhered to microscope slides pre-coated with Poly-L-lysine (Yuanye, China). Slides were blocked with QuickBlock™ Immune Staining Blocking Buffer (Beyotime, China). Purified HopQ protein was then incubated with the cells for 1 hour. After washing, bound HopQ was detected by incubating with a primary His Tag Mouse Monoclonal Antibody (Beyotime, China) (1:500 dilution) for 1 hour. Slides were washed and incubated with a secondary Cy3-conjugated Goat Anti-Mouse IgG (H+L) (Beyotime, China) (1:1000 dilution) for 1 hour in the dark. The yeast cell wall was counterstained with Calcofluor White stain (Yuanye, China). Slides were mounted and observed under a fluorescence microscope.
