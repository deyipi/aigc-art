#### Sensory System

##### Plasmid Construction

Coding sequences for GPCRs and GPCR-P2A-Protein constructs were PCR-amplified from cloning plasmids (human GPCRs) or synthesized (chimeric GPCRs and terminal complexes), and then inserted in the expression vector pESC-HIS-pGAP-αMF by In-fusion cloning. Coding sequence for chimeric G-proteins were synthesized and inserted in the expression vector pESC-URA-MatRep-pGAP by In-fusion cloning.

PCRs were performed using BeyoFusion PCR Master Mix (2X) (Beyotime, China). The PCR product was run on a 1% agarose gel in 1x TAE buffer, cut out, and extracted using the DNA Gel Extraction Kit (Beyotime, China).The In-fusion assembly was conducted in 10 µL total volume with 10µL of 2x ClonExpress Ultra One Step Cloning Kit V3 (Vazyme Biotech, China). 

In-fusion products were respectively transformed into *Escherichia coli* DH5α competent cells. Transformants were selected using colony PCR with testing primers. Plasmids were then extracted from these positive clones and verified by sequencing to ensure the sequences were 100% correct, The matched plasmids were then transformed into the chassis yeast strain using Competent Cell Preparation and Transformation Kit for *Saccharomyces Cerevisiae* (Beyotime, China), selected, and scaled-up for further tests.

##### GPCR function validation
The experiment utilized SD-His-Ura and SD-His liquid media to culture *S. cerevisiae* transformed with different plasmid combinations. Sterile 1× PBS (Beyotime, China) and 100 mM dATP(Beyotime, China) were prepared, along with histamine(Innochem, China) gradient solutions at concentrations of 0, 30 nM, 120 nM, 600 nM, 3 μM, and 15 μM (labeled as 0-5), and dATP gradient solutions (prepared by mixing 8 μL of 100 mM dATP with 92 μL PBS to obtain Solution 3, followed by twofold serial dilution to obtain Solutions 2 and 1, with PBS as Solution 0). 

The entire experiment was performed under sterile conditions, with the enzyme plate reader pre-adjusted in the UV-sterilized biosafety cabinet. After overnight pre-cultivation of the yeast strains, the cells were gently resuspended, and 140 μL of yeast suspension and 10 μL of the specified reagent were added to each well according to the 96-well plate layout. After sealing the plate, the "GPCR×FG Validation" protocol was selected, the plate layout adjusted, and "Use Plate Lid" was checked. The plate was correctly positioned, and fluorescence detection was initiated.
